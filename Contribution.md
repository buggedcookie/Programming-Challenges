# Contributing Guidelines

Thanks for your interest in contributing!

## How to Contribute

### Improving Existing Solutions

Feel free to suggest improvements or optimizations to my existing code. Remember, my solutions are personal attempts and most of the time messy, your input is highly appreciated!

### Adding New Challenges or Ideas

If you have new challenge ideas, please add them in one of the existing categories:

- **Programs** (anything that isn't a full application or game)
- **Applications**
- **Games**
- **Miscellaneous**

You’re also welcome to suggest new categories, just keep them reasonably broad and avoid overly specific categories.

### Writing Guides or Explanations

When adding a guide or explanation for a challenge, please:

- Place your guide inside the challenge's folder.

- Name it `ReadMe.md` or `Guide.md`.

- Include any sources or reference URLs you used.
  
  > Don’t use URL shorteners. Always link to full, direct URLs from well-known and trustworthy sources.

- Use **as much inference and visualization as possible**, don't just say "do this and that". Explain your reasoning, assumptions, steps, anything that can help.
  
  > There’s nothing more frustrating than a tutorial that just says “do this and that” without explaining why. Sure, some people are fine with that, but most are watching a tutorial because they *don’t* understand the concept yet. A good guide should explain the *why*, *when*, not just the *how*.

- Break down concepts clearly, assuming readers might be beginners or new to the concept presented.

---

## What Not to Do

- Please **do not add fully completed solutions** as pull requests.
  
  > This repo mainly tracks my own progress on these challenges. It’s public so others can browse and get inspiration.

- Avoid overcomplicating explanations; **clarity** and **simplicity** are **key**.
  
  > Even though I encourage explanations to include inference and detailed reasoning, if the explanation gets too complicated or confusing, it misses the point. Always aim to balance depth with clarity.
